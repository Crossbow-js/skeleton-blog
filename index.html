How we work at @wearejh

For the developers and designers at JH, implementing and adhering to both a high quality of code and a consistence style of code is an important & challenging task. We have tooling in place that ensures any new techniques or industry best practices can be integrated into our workflow (via automated builds and development tools) with the least amount of friction possible.

We love iterating on our existing processes and do not shy away from ditching much-loved tools and software should we be introduced to that new, shinier tool that can do the job better!

In fact, we believe so deeply in creating productive environments for our teams that we regularly contribute to and even have our own open source projects such as BrowserSync.
